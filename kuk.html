    About TRINITY

    TRINITY Pharmaceuticals Private Limited is one of the top THIRD-PARTY INJECTABLE PHARMA MANUFACTURER & SUPPLIERS, with a mission to bring good health to the people of India with its exclusive & extensive product range. We manufacture and supply products in Human as well as Veterinary Animal Range Injections in all over India. Since its inception in 1996, TRINITY Pharma International has been involved in developing a growing portfolio of best-in-class pharmaceutical injectables formulations in the healthcare arena. TRINITY Pharma International has been involved in the manufacturing and development of best-in-class pharmaceutical products in the healthcare arena. Our company is a large and dynamic group that marks a leading position in the field of pharmaceutical formulations Our company has built a reputation for excellence and superiority based on offering consistently high quality, constantly expanding product portfolio, on-time deliveries, and state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities. With the help of modern technology and high-tech machinery installed in our production area, we can manufacture a vast range of drugs in the shortest period. By following the guidelines laid by the WHO and with the help of GMP & ISO certified units, the company offers Exclusive manufacturing Deals in all types of Human & Veterinary Injections (Dry and Liquid) which include Beta-lactum, non beta-lactum, Carbapenems, Cephalosporins, Liquid Injectables, Eye/Ear/ Nasal Drops, and Sprays, Bacteria virus, Digestion issue, liver infection bacteria disease and many more. TRINITY Pharma International has its Headquarters in karnal, India with manufacturing unit facilities based at KARNAL RAMWAR ROAD (Haryana). Currently, the Company offers more than 100+ products & molecules, which are manufactured under different brand names for their clients in all over India, which are tested at our own In House Quality Control Laboratories and manufacturing plants. We manufacture vast range of pharmaceutical injectables at the best prices of the industry so that every individual can easily afford them. The company has strong ties up with the best networking teams that help us to stipulate all the orders to our customers as per our commitments to them. Being a customer-oriented company, we do not compromise on quality, which has enabled us to gain a niche position for ourselves in the industry.
